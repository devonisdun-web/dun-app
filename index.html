What DUN does
Stop worrying about whether you can afford the right support. Start making sure you're getting it.

DUN prepares legal documents for women who are self-represented or working alongside an attorney. Divorce, legal separation, custody agreements, parenting plans, child support worksheets, orders of protection, and full court document organization.

You do not need to know what any of that means yet. That is what the first conversation is for.

How it works
Three steps. No surprises.

You tell me what's going on. In your own words. I read every word personally.

I send you a plan and a price. No commitment until you are ready.

We get to work. Contract, deposit, and we handle every document.

What it costs
Most cases average $500 to $1,000.

You will know the full cost before you commit to anything. If cost is a concern, tell me. We will figure it out.

Confidentiality
Everything you share comes directly to me.
